Cancellation policy

We understand that circumstances may change. Please read our cancellation policy to ensure you are aware of any fees or restrictions that may apply.

Cancellation request

To proceed with canceling your order, please enter your order number in the field provided and select a reason for the cancellation from the dropdown menu.

Order number

Please enter the unique order number associated with the purchase you wish to cancel. This helps us locate and process your request promptly.

Reason for cancellation

Select the reason for canceling your order from the list. This information helps us improve our services and address any issues you may have encountered.

Confirmation

Once you submit your cancellation request, you will receive a confirmation email with details about the status of your cancellation. Please allow some time for processing.

Contact us

If you have any questions or need further assistance with your cancellation request, please reach out to our customer support team via email or phone. We are here to help.
